## OBJECTIVE

Understand why paying customers cancel in their first ninety days, well enough to rank the causes.

## APPROACH

Pull the cancellation cohort from the billing exports, interview the five most recent cancellations, and cross-reference support tickets for the cohort. Weight findings by revenue, not seat count.
